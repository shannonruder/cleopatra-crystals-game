Cleopatra's Crystals Challenge

INSTRUCTIONS

Make an offering to Cleopatra by meeting her crystals quota! Figure out the secret value of each crystal and use any combination to add them up to her quota. To begin click on one or more of the gems. If your score goes over her quota, you lose the game. Successfully collecting crystals that add up to Cleopatra's quota and you win. Collecting too many crystals that goes over her quota will cause you to lose.
